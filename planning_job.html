Planning Your Job & Internship Search

Why Planning Matters

A well-structured job or internship search can save you time, reduce stress, and increase your chances of success. By setting goals and tracking your progress, youâ€™ll stay focused and organized throughout the process.

Steps to Plan Your Search

  1. Self-Assessment: Identify your strengths, skills, and career interests.
  2. Research: Explore industries, companies, and roles that align with your goals.
  3. Timeline: Map out deadlines for applications, networking, and interviews.
  4. Materials: Prepare your resume, cover letter, and LinkedIn profile.
  5. Track Progress: Use a spreadsheet or tool to keep track of applications.
